The math
$1,000 on TikTok buys ~143k impressions — but at ~25% completion, only ~36k viewers see your full message. The same $1,000 on NFL CTV buys ~18k impressions at 95%+ completion = ~17k completed views. Factor in 66% higher search lift (EDO) and 2× ad recall vs mobile video (Comcast Advertising), and the cost per engaged viewer is competitive — with dramatically higher impact per exposure.

Methodology. CPM ranges from Gupta Media Social CPM Tracker (Oct 2025), Streamlayer CTV Guide (Jan 2026), IAB 2025 Digital Video Ad Spend Report. Completion rates from Innovid 2024 Global Benchmarks (CTV: 95%+), SEO Design Chicago (YouTube: 20–40%), industry averages (social: 15–30%). Ad effectiveness from EDO 2024–25 studies. Demographics from S&P Global (NFL age skew), Nielsen Tops of Sports 2025 (NBA diversity, MLB), The Shelf/Nielsen Scarborough (NBA avg age, HHI), EMI Research (income tiers by league), CivicScience/One Elevate (F1 US demos), F1 2025 Global Fan Survey (gender/age), Quantumrun (NFL gender).
